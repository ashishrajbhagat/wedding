How We Met

The Swipe That Stuck!

Like every modern-day love story, ours began... on a matrimonial site! 💻💌 Yep, the place where algorithms 🤖 work harder than Cupid himself 🏹. Little did we know, amidst all the profiles, we’d stumble upon each other – the perfect match 😍 (or so we thought after scanning through each other’s bio at least three times 👀).

The first message? Polite. 😊 The second? Intriguing. 🤔 By the third message, we were already wondering how the universe 🌌 managed to connect two souls who’d never met before. One month of endless chats 💬, quirky jokes 😂, and discovering just how much we don’t like pineapple on pizza 🍍❌🍕 later, we both came to a conclusion: “Hey, this just might work!”

So here we are, turning that one-month conversation into a lifetime of adventure 🚀. Turns out, love 💖 can find you when you least expect it – even if it comes in the form of a matrimonial profile with a slightly awkward photo! 📸🥰
